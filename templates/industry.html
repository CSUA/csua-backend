Industry
The Berkeley CSUA works closely with companies, investors, entrepreneurs, and active members of the software industry to bring both mentorship and job opportunities to students.
Cause
The CSUA is dedicated to improving the experience of computer science undergraduates at Berkeley. We host hackathons, where students hack on projects for 18-hour long coding periods, as well as other events, bringing industry speakers to campus. We also provide free UNIX logins and server hosting for students. Our study and social lounges are places for students to relax and study.
Donate with the full confidence that your donation will be used to support a new generation of Berkeley engineers.
Contributions
If you would like to contribute to the CSUA, please email the Politburo at politburo@csua.berkeley.edu. Thank you for your generosity!
If you are a company representative and would like to sponsor a CSUA event or donate for a particular purpose, please contact our industrial relations VP at indrel@csua.berkeley.edu.
